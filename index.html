About MindTap

Many mobile applications assume that users can rely on touch, fine motor control, or voice input. For individuals with physical disabilities, these assumptions can create significant barriers to digital independence.

MindTap was created to challenge those assumptions. By leveraging electroencephalography (EEG) headsets, the application enables hands-free interaction with smartphones, offering an alternative pathway for control that does not depend on physical movement.
