Our Project

Innovative Neurotechnology Solution

MindTap combines hardware, software, and thoughtful design to create a practical and ethical neurotechnology solution.

EEG-based Interaction

Brain signal patterns are interpreted and mapped to mobile inputs, allowing users to navigate and interact without touch. Our advanced signal processing algorithms translate neural activity into intuitive device controls.

Accessible Interface Design

The application supports screen readers, keyboard navigation, high-contrast visuals, and reduced-motion preferences. We've built accessibility into every aspect of the user experience, ensuring that MindTap is usable by everyone.

Privacy-conscious Approach

The project emphasizes responsible data handling and transparency when working with sensitive neural data. We implement strict privacy protocols and give users full control over their data.

Real-world Motivation

The design decisions are guided by real accessibility challenges, not hypothetical use cases. We work directly with users to understand their needs and create solutions that make a meaningful difference in their daily lives.
